Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Do I need an appointment for an estimate?

No appointment needed. Stop by during business hours and we'll take a look at your vehicle and give you a free, straightforward estimate on the spot.

Can you match my vehicle's paint color?

Yes. We use your vehicle's factory color code to mix and match paint precisely. Spot repairs are blended into surrounding panels so the result looks factory-original.

How long does a typical repair take?

It depends on the work involved. Minor repairs may be done in a day or two. Larger jobs like full resprays or significant collision damage may take longer — we'll give you a timeline with the estimate.

Can I send photos before coming in?

Absolutely. Text or email us photos of the damage and we can give you a rough idea of what's involved. It's a good way to start before making the drive over.

Do you repair bumpers, trim, and body panels?

Yes — bumpers, doors, quarter panels, fenders, trim, and more. Whether it's from a collision or just wear over time, we handle all body and trim repairs.

Do you work with insurance claims?

We recommend calling the shop directly to discuss your situation. Every insurance claim is different, and Luis can help you understand your options and the best next step.
