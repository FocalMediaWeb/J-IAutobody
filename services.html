What We Offer

Our Services

Eight services, one shop. Everything your car needs to look its best.

01

Body & Trim

Collision damage, rust repair, panel replacement, and trim restoration — we handle everything from fender benders to more serious structural work. We prep and finish every surface properly so the repair holds and looks right for years.

  • Collision damage and panel replacement
  • Rust repair and surface preparation
  • Trim, molding, and door handle restoration
Collision Repair
02

Painting

Whether it's a single panel or a full vehicle respray, we mix and match your exact color code for a seamless result. We use quality automotive paint with proper clear-coat protection so the finish looks factory-fresh and resists fading.

  • Full respray matched to factory color code
  • Spot and panel painting with color blending
  • Clear-coat protection for lasting finish
Color Matching
03

Paintless Dent Removal

Hail damage, parking lot dings, and minor dents can often be removed without any paint work at all. Our PDR process massages the metal back into shape from behind the panel, preserving your factory finish completely.

  • Hail damage on roofs, hoods, and panels
  • Door dings and parking lot dents
  • No filler, no repainting — original finish preserved
No Paint Required
04

Decals

From business lettering to full-panel graphics, we cut and install vinyl decals cleanly and precisely. We take the time to get alignment right so the result looks intentional, not applied.

  • Custom vinyl graphics and lettering
  • Fleet and commercial vehicle branding
  • Precise installation and alignment
Custom Vinyl
05

Pin Stripes

A pin stripe done right is a clean, thin accent that makes the lines of a vehicle pop. We apply pinstriping by tape or by hand, to your choice of width and color. It's a small detail that makes a big difference.

  • Hand-applied or tape pinstripe work
  • Single and multi-color stripe options
  • Any vehicle type or body style
Detailing
06

Fiberglass Repair

Cracked bumpers, fiberglass body kits, and composite panels require a different approach than sheet metal. We rebuild damaged fiberglass to the original contour and finish it so the repair is invisible.

  • Cracks, chips, and breaks fully rebuilt
  • Bumpers, body kits, and custom panels
  • Finished to match surrounding paint exactly
Composite & Bumpers
07

Wheel Painting

Curb rash is one of the most common cosmetic problems on any car. We repair the damage, prep the wheel properly, and respray it in the color and finish of your choice — gloss, satin, matte, or custom.

  • Curb rash and scuff repair
  • Custom color refinish in any finish type
  • Gloss, satin, matte — your choice
Custom Finish
08

Headlight Restoration

Cloudy, yellowed headlights cut your night visibility and make the car look older than it is. We sand, polish, and apply a UV-protective seal so your lenses come out crystal clear and stay that way.

  • Removes yellowing and hazing from lenses
  • UV sealant to prevent quick re-clouding
  • Improves nighttime visibility and appearance
Visibility & Appearance
